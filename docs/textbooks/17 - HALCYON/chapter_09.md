He had written that the architecture, on his honest internal accounting eleven days after the cascade, *could still be deployed correctly somewhere by someone, and that the somewhere-and-someone work was the work he wanted to spend the rest of his professional life doing.*

He had mailed the letter to Theodore's PO box in West Plains on the twenty-eighth of November of Year Thirteen.
